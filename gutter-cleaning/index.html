GUTTER CLEANING

Clogged gutters can lead to water damage, pest infestations, and costly repairs. At Window Hero, our gutter cleaning services are designed to keep rainwater flowing freely and away from your home. We remove debris like leaves, sticks, and dirt using commercial-grade tools, including extendable scoops, high-powered vacuums, and hand-cleaning methods where needed.

GUTTER CLEANING IN SWINDON

Blocked gutters can cause serious problems, from leaks and damp patches to structural damage. Our gutter clearing service removes leaves, moss, and debris using powerful, high-reach vacuums — no ladders required.

Why clear your gutters?

  • Prevent water overflow and leaks.
  • Protect brickwork and foundations.
  • Avoid costly repairs in the future.

We work safely from the ground and can even provide before and after photos so you can see the results.
